Arin Ilejay
Arin Ilejay is the newest member of the band; taking over the full-time place of the former Rev. After the Rev's passing the band needed a new drummer to take position for their drummer long-term. One of the band's producers knew of Mr. Ilejay from recording a previous album with the studio. Arin's techinical drumming is what placed him into the shoes of the Rev for the band.
