Synyster Gates
Synyster Gates, or Brian Haner, Jr., is the lead guitarist for the band. He also is one of the backing vocalists and plays a wide variety of intruments along with most of the other members. Syn is known mostly for his styling appearance and his insanely creative guitar solos. Synyster Gate's father, Brian Haner, Sr., is widely known internationally as "The Guitar Guy".
